Theatre, Film and Digital Production🎥

My name is Ayden Prenza. I moved from New York to Los Angeles when I was about 10 years old. Growing up in New York taught me many things and it has shaped me into the person that I am today. My love for film making and movie writing was developed throughout the course of my life because of my dad. My dad and I would stay up all night together binge watching Bruce Lee movies, The Matrix, all of the Marvel movies, and other martial arts and action films. I also grew up around lots of horror movies like Halloween, Friday the 13th, Hellraiser, jigsaw, and IT. When I moved to Los Angeles it only amplified my desire to be in the film industry because I would see all of the movies I recognized at Hollywood and feel star struck just by seeing them. I want to keep my eyes open for opportunities that will help sharpen my skills as a film maker. I hope to find work starting out as a PA, helping around sets and preparing things for the crew to ensure that recording goes as smoothly as possible. I found my love for writing in high school and it was mostly due to my English and writing classes. While in school, I enjoyed writing about subjects that I was very passionate about. I also enjoyed reading comic books which led to my imagination being further developed and broadened into something that would help push not only my artistic capabilities but my writing capabilities as well. I enjoy creating art for original characters and characters that already exist. I find that drawing helps me think outside the box and solve problems which helps me sharpen my creative skills. I took AP art classes and AP English classes in high school in order to further sharpen my skills and I found that it was something I really enjoyed. This led me into wanting to create films because as a director, I would not only be able to create art with my camera work, but I would also be able to write compelling stories that can inspire young minds just like me. My greatest inspirations for film making are Sam Rami, Clive Barker, Matt Reaves, James Gunn, and David FIncher. Their writing inspires me to write compelling stories whether it be horror, action, mystery, thriller, or superhero. I aspire to be a great director someday and I hope that I can build a strong resume filled with accolades by the time that I am ready to be hired for a project. Thank you for your time and consideration.
